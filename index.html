A PM who leads
with questions,
not answers.

I started as a full stack developer who couldn't stop asking "but why does the user do that?" That curiosity pulled me into product management where I spent years inside funnel data, finding the exact moments users gave up.

It was rarely what anyone expected. Identity verification failures weren't user errors they were API timeouts. Funding drop offs weren't cold feet they were specific payment processor failures. The data always had an answer. My job was asking the right question first.

Every funnel is a hypothesis, not a given.
Ship small, learn fast, measure everything.
Support tickets are my favorite research tool.
